







# Sample Request for Proposal (RFP)

## Project Overview
The ABC Construction Company is seeking proposals from qualified contractors to build a new 5-story office building in downtown Metro City. The project includes:

- Total floor area: 10,000 sq ft per floor
- Structural system: Steel frame with concrete slabs
- Exterior cladding: Glass curtain wall
- Sustainable features: LEED Silver certification target

## Scope of Work
1. **Site Preparation**: Excavation, grading, foundation work
2. **Structural Construction**: Steel framing, concrete pouring
3. **MEP Systems**: Electrical, plumbing, HVAC installation
4. **Interior Finishes**: Drywall, flooring, painting
5. **Exterior Work**: Glass curtain wall installation

## Deliverables
- Detailed cost estimate
- Project timeline (Gantt chart)
- Sustainability analysis report
- Compliance certification plan
